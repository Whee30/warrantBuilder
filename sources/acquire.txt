In addition to the seizure of the physical electronic items mentioned above, investigating officers as well as agents acting under their direction are authorized to complete an off-site search of the seized items for evidence of the crime(s) described. This search includes the acquisition of data from the seized device(s) and the processing of the acquired data in order to search for evidence of the crime(s) described. In some rare circumstances, potentially destructive processes such as JTAG, ISP and/or Chip-Off acquisitions may be necessary to acquire data from seized devices. In the event such methods are deemed necessary, investigating officers as well as agents acting under their direction are authorized to employ these potentially destructive techniques.
