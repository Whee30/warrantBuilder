Laptop/Desktop Computers. Investigating officers are authorized, at their discretion, to seize all “computer systems” believed to contain some or all of the evidence described in the warrant, to include necessary accessories such as power cords, manuals, supporting documentation, and related input devices deemed necessary to recover digital evidence from the seized systems. In addition to the computer systems, investigating officers are authorized, at their discretion, to seize internal/external HDDs, SSDs, SD cards, MicroSD cards, USB drives, CDs, Floppy Disks, Tape drives, other digital storage media, and Routers which are believed to contain some or all of the evidence described in the warrant, as well as associated accessories deemed necessary to facilitate the acquisition of data from the seized items.
