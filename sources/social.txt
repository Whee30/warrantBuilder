Social media account information. Investigating officers are authorized to obtain information related to the listed social media account(s). This information includes:
  User name(s)
  Real name(s)
  Address(es)
  Email Address(es)
  Account creation date
  IP logs for account usage
  Private Messages to include embedded/associated media files to/from the listed account(s)
  Group Messages to include embedded/associated media files to/from the listed account(s)
  Posts/Stories made by the listed account(s)
  Comments made by the listed account(s) on other posts/stories
  Location information associated with the listed account(s)
  Stored methods of payment
  Device information associated with the listed account(s)
  Apple App Store and/or Google Play Store identifiers associated with the listed account(s)
